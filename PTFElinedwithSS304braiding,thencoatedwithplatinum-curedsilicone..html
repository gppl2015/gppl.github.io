PTFE lined with SS 304 braiding,
then coated with platinum-cured silicone.
SanTef features PTFE tubing reinforced with SS 304 braid and enveloped in a layer of top-tier Platinum silicone. This configuration ensures its ability to withstand high pressures and offers heightened chemical resistance. Its ultra-smooth bore promotes superior flow properties and minimizes adhesion to particulates. Furthermore, the silicone outer layer acts as insulation against high temperatures and maintains surfaces dust-free.
